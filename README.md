# Curly Adventure

## Description

Curly Adventure is a 2D platformer where you play as a dog exploring themed levels, dodging hazards, and battling enemies.

Your goal in each level is to collect at least 70% of coins and gems to unlock the exit door. Along the way, you can find hidden letters to spell **BONES**, break crates for loot, collect power-ups, and chase a higher score.

## How to Play

## [Play Game](https://bhayanak.github.io/curly-adventure/)

### Objective

1. Move through the level and collect coins and gems.
2. Reach at least 70% collectible progress to unlock the exit.
3. Enter the unlocked door to clear the level.
4. Keep lives above zero to avoid game over.

### Keyboard Controls

- Move: `Left/Right Arrow` or `A/D`
- Jump / Double Jump: `Space` or `Up Arrow` or `W`
- Crouch: `Down Arrow` or `S`
- Run: `Shift`
- Dash: `X` or `K`
- Punch: `F` or `J`
- Pause: `P` or `Esc`
- Mute: `M`
- Start/Continue prompt: `Enter`

### Tips

- Use run + jump to clear longer pits.
- Dash and punch are useful for enemies and crates.
- Hearts restore health, eggs grant extra lives.
- Hidden letters that spell **BONES** give bonus rewards.
